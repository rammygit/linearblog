Welcome to GSGL (Gujarati Samaj of Greater Louisville)

The GSGL (Gujarati Samaj of Greater Louisville), was founded in 1990. GSGL was formed to bring together all Louisville and near by cities Gujarati people to promote and preserve Gujarati culture, heritage, language, social and cultural awareness by providing a platform to socialize and network within community while serving the community needs.
